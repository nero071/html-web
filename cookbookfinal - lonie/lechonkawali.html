Lechon Kawali Recipe

Introduction

Lechon Kawali is a popular Filipino dish known for its crispy, deep-fried pork belly. It's a favorite for special occasions and is typically served with a side of liver sauce or vinegar dipping sauce. This recipe guides you through making this deliciously crunchy and tender pork dish at home.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs pork belly
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 4 cups water
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)
  • Liver sauce or vinegar dipping sauce (for serving)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, combine pork belly, salt, pepper, garlic powder, sliced onion, minced garlic, bay leaves, soy sauce, and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for about 1 hour, or until the pork is tender.
  2. Remove the pork from the pot and let it cool completely. Pat it dry with paper towels to remove excess moisture.
  3. Heat a large amount of vegetable oil in a deep pan or skillet over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, carefully add the pork belly, frying in batches if necessary, until golden brown and crispy, about 5-7 minutes per side.
  4. Remove the pork from the oil and drain on paper towels.
  5. Serve hot with liver sauce or vinegar dipping sauce.
