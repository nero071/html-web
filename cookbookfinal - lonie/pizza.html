Classic Pizza Recipe

Introduction

Classic pizza is a universally loved dish with a crispy crust, savory tomato sauce, and gooey mozzarella cheese. This recipe is straightforward and perfect for making a delicious homemade pizza from scratch. Customize it with your favorite toppings for a personalized touch!

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni (optional)
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms (optional)
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives (optional)
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat up.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness. Transfer the dough to a pizza peel or baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce, then add the grated Parmesan cheese.
  5. Add any additional toppings like pepperoni, mushrooms, and olives, if desired.
  6. Season the pizza with dried oregano, basil, and garlic powder. Drizzle a small amount of olive oil over the top.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  8. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired, then slice and serve.
