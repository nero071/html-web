Crispy French Fry

Introduction

They say money can't buy happiness, but it can buy French fries, and that's pretty much the same this. French fries: the ultimate temptation. ...

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ pounds russet potatoes, peeled
  • 1 cup vegetable oil for frying
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1 teaspoon onion salt
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup water, or as needed
  • 1 cup buttermilk

Instructions

  1. Slice the potatoes and place them in cold water to prevent browning.
  2. Sift the flour and seasonings, then gradually stir in water to make a batter.
  3. Drain and dry the sliced potatoes, then dip in the batter.
  4. Fry the potatoes until golden brown and crispy.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy with seasonings and ketchup sauce for dipping.
